Real-World O2C Implementation — Coca-Cola Bottlers & SAP

Concise case study showing how Coca-Cola bottlers integrated SAP (S/4HANA & SAP Commerce Cloud) to improve order capture, delivery tracking, automated billing and cash application — results and references included for recruiters and interview panels.

Business Context (Coca-Cola HBC & Bottlers)

Coca-Cola Hellenic Bottling Company (Coca-Cola HBC) and several Coca-Cola bottlers worldwide implemented SAP S/4HANA / SAP Commerce Cloud to centralize order capture, inventory visibility, logistics, and invoicing. These efforts created a unified digital order management backbone for thousands of B2B customers and millions of small orders per year.

End-to-End Flow

Process Steps (O2C)

1
Inquiry & Quotation
Customer request, pricing & validity entered (SD / Commerce Cloud).
2
Sales Order (SO)
SO created; customer master and pricing conditions applied (SD).
3
Availability & Credit Check
ATP checks inventory; credit policies evaluated (SD + MM + FI-AR).
4
Delivery & Picking
Outbound delivery, pick/pack, route planning; integrated with transport visibility tools.
5
Post Goods Issue (PGI)
Inventory reduced; COGS and accounting postings triggered (MM / FI-CO).
6
Billing & Invoice
Invoice generation with tax; accounting doc posted; SD→FI integration.
7
Payment & Clearing
Bank statements, auto-matching, dunning, dispute resolution (FI-AR).
